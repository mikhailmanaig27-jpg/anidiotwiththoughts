An idiot with thoughts

You know the part where even if you try to do things you still feel the same? You are trapped in a loop of repeating the same day even if you keep trying new hobbies, actions, work, etc. At this age of 17 thats exactly how I feel. I dont have everything figured out. I dont even know what im doing, even now im writing this website, practicing coding while drinking coffee yet it still feels the same. I know im not the only one experiencing this, well someone has it worse than me but Im just here to share my thoughts while protraying myself as an idiot.

I have been feeling alone lately. I can reach out to some "people" for help but theres this part of me where i dont wanna be a burden to others. Ive also been judged as weak and emotional by even those who are close to me, thats another reason. I grew up feeling alone and handling things alone. I kept getting left out at school and trying everything I can just to fit into this society where you cant be yourself, just remembering it makes me sick. Even before I didnt have any friend group my whole life. I was tired of walking alone and going home alone. I know bottling up emotions lead to something worse so I acknowledge myself time to time but these past few months I've been on autopilot. Trying to focus on my short term goals while trying to avoid overthinking where my life might lead me. Yet I still feel the same dreadful feeling thats been haunting me for months.

I like to imagine this dark figure thats following me throughout my life. This dark figure is what makes me feel that dreadful feeling which leads me to spiral in depression and makes me "worthless", thats what I like to call it. Im used to calling myself a useless idiot who doesnt know what he is doing. Yeah I try to do shit but all I get in return is "What are you doing?". Just hearing that kills my confidence. I mean yeah I do not know what im doing but cant they just understand the part where I TRIED???.

I often try to disappear but a part of me keeps hoping that someones out there waiting for me, I kept looking out for people, motivating them, supporting them and even smiling to strangers. I dont know why but when I do, I keep hoping that ill receive the same treatment from them which I clearly dont. Thats the part that hurts me the most. The worst part is I dont regret it. I dont know if im just a caring person or a person with no self respect. That part confuses me. I just accept things while my chest hurts because for me, asking to receive the same treatment is just too much to ask for. I always joke around, smiling and being a "clown" for others. Another thing is when Im comfortable to people, I act like a kid. Ive been judged about this and it hurt me deeply. Its like I cant even be myself to people whom Ive given my Inner child to. Maybe thats just life.

Being pressured to be successful in life also did a number on me. The thought of being more useless than I already am makes me scared. I have skills in computer, guitar, small knowledge, etc but not being able to figure out what to do is what makes me doubt myself. I wish I was passionate about something which what people my age already do, they have their life figured out, they know what they want yet im here being on autopilot. Im slowly trying to change that mindset of "whatever happens" to "this is what I want". I guess it will help me somewhat shift in life. Noticing im not the same level as people on my current age also makes me doubt myself, be it body, skills, etc. I know some people are meant to bloom early but it just makes me wonder what am I capable of. I have my own life which I kept private. Only people close to me such as my girlfriend and family knows me but what I know for sure is "no one knows me as much as myself". I admit I hide things because Im afraid to be judged again. Ive been judged for my body, motives in life, etc and It made me feel worthless, but what hurts me the most is the part that they dont notice im TRYING.

Not to mention my family is already falling apart as my mother and father are on opposite edges. My family is emotionally absent, I cant talk to anyone properly. Seeing their actions makes me wonder why I was even born in the first place. Im not supposed to handle this at my age, I would rather die than watch my family fall apart for stupid reasons. No adults are guiding me in my age, old online friends are the only ones I have to ask for advice just to know more in life. Sound stupid right?. I like to blame the devil for these reasons.

I guess some things kept me going through these past few days, My morning coffee especially. Sounds funny but even a small cup of coffee gives me comfort that im somewhat "okay". Pushups, gives me somewhat little confidence being able to do 30 everyday. Xxxtentacion saved me more than people around me did. His music sat beside me rather than helping me and thats what gave me the feeling that Im not alone. My girlfriend, sometimes were blurry but I still love my girl. The first person who stayed through me while knowing im not fully developed. Yet despite these things im still tired of eating alone everyday.

Basic Summary of this idiot: Im afraid of being judged, Im always there but no ones there for me, Im scared of my future, Im insecure about everything, I dont know what Im doing, I have no one to talk to, I cant be myself in this society, I have to force myself to keep my head up, I have to think twice before speaking, I have to keep pretending, Im fucking SCARED.

What people notice on me is what I look like on the outside, how I act, what I look like and how I speak. They dont notice whats inside me, they dont try to UNDERSTAND why I am like this, even by those closest to me but fuck that. The society we live in doesnt give a shit about me. The world will walk over you any chance it can so I have to keep my ground and keep my head up. Im still here, existing despite everything. I guess thats an advantage.

Im not sure if this is just a phase or if this is how life is. I just want to be happy, I want to be loved, I want to be accepted, I want to be understood, I want to be appreciated, I want to be cared for, I want to be supported, I want to be motivated, I want to be respected, I want to be treated equally and most of all, I just want to be myself. Is that too much to ask for? Maybe it is. Maybe its just me being an idiot who has no idea what he wants. Maybe im just a useless idiot who doesnt know what hes doing. Maybe im just a worthless idiot who has no future. Maybe I need to give myself some more time. Maybe im just an idiot with thoughts.
